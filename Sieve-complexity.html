Sieve of Eratosthenes is an ancient algorithm introduced by Greek mathematician Eratosthenes in the third century BC, used to find prime numbers up to a given number. In this material we will discuss this algorithm and determine the exact number of eliminations made.

Algorithm:

  1. List all integers from 2 to nnn﻿
  1. Start with the smallest prime, set p=2 p = 2p=2﻿
  1. Eliminate all the proper multiples of ppp﻿ which are less than nnn﻿ such as 2p,3p,4p,...2p, 3p, 4p, . . .2p,3p,4p,...﻿
  1. Assign the value of ppp﻿ to the smallest number next greater than ppp﻿ which have been not eliminated, remove the multiples of that number
  1. Repeat the steps until p≤n p \leq \sqrt{n} p≤n​﻿
